January 1, 2024 - Lee Seung-ro, Mayor of Seongbuk-gu
The hopeful new year of 2024 (Year of the Blue Dragon) has dawned.

There is a saying that in a new time, we must have a new heart.
In this new time, if there is excitement that makes our hearts beat with anticipation, then in our new hearts, there will be a steadfast commitment that strengthens us.

As the District Mayor of Seongbuk-gu, in this hopeful new year, I, too, renew my commitment to take responsibility for the lives of our 430,000 Seongbuk-gu residents.

In 2024, I am committed to upholding the fundamentals and principles across all aspects of district administration. With more open communication and improved policies, I will strive to make Seongbuk-gu a better place to live.

As we embark on the new year, in the warm-hearted community of Seongbuk-gu, I hope that, through renewed resolve and new challenges, all Seongbuk-gu residents will achieve their dreams.

Respected residents!
In the coming year, I will continue to walk alongside you in the heart of your lives, in village entrances and neighborhood alleys.

May this new year bring more love to give and receive.

January 1, 2024 Seongbuk-gu District Mayor, Lee Seung-ro
