Sunrise Event

A special festival for Seongbuk-gu residents to wish for the health and happiness of their families.

  • Date Every New Year’s Day (Jan. 1)
  • Highlights Traditional performances, drum shows, sunrise viewing, etc.
  • Contact Culture and Sports Division (+82) 2-2241-2603
  • Location Gaeunsan Park Field
View Map

On New Year's Day, residents come together with family, friends, and neighbors to share well-wishes, set hopeful plans for the year, and reaffirm their resolutions. As they wait for the year’s first sunrise, they can enjoy a variety of cultural performances.
