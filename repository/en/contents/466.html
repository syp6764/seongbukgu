Gaeunsan Sports Center

“Gaeunsan Sports Center” is a dynamic sporting complex designed to bring energy and vitality from its surrounding natural environment to your life

  • Inquiries Tel. 02-925 - 9960~2 / Fax. 02-925-9963

The Gaeunsan Sports Center features a clean water swimming center at the heart of the busy city. It has spacious and comfortable indoor venues, and offers regular basic medical check-ups and physical analysis using the latest Bio Space body composition tester (Inbody 4.0) in connection with local hospitals. The Dynamic Fitness Center provides optimized exercise prescriptions, an aerobic running room where you can enjoy exercise comfortably, and a variety of recreational activity programs. The Gaeunsan Sports Center also helps younger members take a break from their studies to enjoy a wide range of hobbies, and older members a chance to pursue their lifelong dreams of playing an instrument or sport. These sports and hobby classes are available for small groups only to ensure better overall quality. You can learn multiple things at the same time thanks to a family-like atmosphere and affordable prices. The Gaeunsan Sports Center also hosts special programs and events on a quarterly basis to make sure it improves its services extended to members.
In terms of amenities, the Center features a sauna that uses natural medicinal herbs, comfortable showers, and a changing room that can accommodate up to 400 people. In addition it has a spacious parking lot with 80 parking spaces on the ground and underground levels. Also, 5 shuttle buses are operated to provide convenience.
Visit the Gaeunsan Sports Center and enjoy its programs designed to promote health as well as its beautiful surrounding landscape for a more relaxed and pleasant lifestyle. The Center promises to take care of everyone’s health with its pleasant facilities and high-quality services.
