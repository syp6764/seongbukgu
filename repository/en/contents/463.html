Seongbuk General Leisure and Sports Town

The Largest Well-Being Landmark in the Northeast Region!

Seongbuk Comprehensive Sports Town is designed to provide an optimal environment for both relaxation and exercise, reflecting modern social trends. It boasts top-quality sports facilities for the best in well-being and fitness.

To ensure ease of access for the community, the center operates on a general membership basis, allowing local residents to use the facilities freely.

Special features include naturally lit swimming and fitness areas, a spacious indoor screen golf range with 27 bays on the 4th and 5th floors (12 screen bays and 15 outdoor-style indoor bays with a 30-yard range), all set in a bright and comfortable environment.
