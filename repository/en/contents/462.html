Swimming Pool

Located on the scenic second floor, this spacious and comfortable pool uses an eco-friendly salt-based electrolysis sterilization system, eliminating the need for chlorine. The electrolysis process generates mixed sanitizing agents that purify the water of harmful substances without using traditional chlorine, ensuring a safe and natural swimming environment. Our dedicated team of instructors provides thorough training, focusing on improving residents’ health and quality of life.

  • Adult Pool 25×13 m, Depth 1.4 m (6 lanes)
  • Children’s Pool 15×5 m, Depth 0.7 m

Golf Range

  • 4F GDR Bays Equipped with 12 GDR screens from Golfzon, offering a top-notch screen training experience.
  • 5F Indoor Bays Features 15 spacious bays with 30-yard driving distance and excellent visibility, a 1,000 y² putting area, and a short-game practice area, all set in a comfortable environment.

Fitness Center

This large, well-ventilated space features an array of cardio equipment (e.g., treadmills, bikes), strength training machines, and auxiliary workout devices, arranged by type for ease of use. Programs for personal and semi-training sessions are tailored for obesity management, adult disease prevention, and physical strength improvement. Free body composition measurements are available, allowing members to monitor their progress easily.

  • Dedicated Area approx. 337 m²Equipment: 22 treadmills among 41 cardio machines, plus 35 types of weight machines and free weights

Squash Court

Located on the first floor, the squash court offers weekly classes (twice per week on weekdays and weekends) to ensure residents enjoy a healthy leisure activity. Led by certified instructors with national and Korean Squash Federation certifications, the program is dedicated to members’ enjoyment and fitness.

  • Courts 2 double-use courts (approx. 205 m²)
