Free Korean Language Education

Some volunteer groups and civic organizations, especially those dealing with migrant laborers, offer free Korean classes.

  • Seoul Global Center

    Seoul Global Center provides Korean language classes for foreigners. Seoul Global Center

  • JOINUS KOREA

    JOINUS KOREA (Multiculuture & Multilingual NGO Under support of Seoul metropolitan city) provides free Korean class for foreigners in Seoul. Free Korean Language Class Sunday

  • Korea Support Center for Foreign Workers

    The Migrant Worker's Welfare Society in Korea offers free Korean semester to help migrant workers better adjust to Korean society. Korea Migrants' center
