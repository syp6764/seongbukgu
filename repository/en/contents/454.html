Local Town Festival

A festival highlighting the unique character of each neighborhood in Seongbuk-gu.

  • Date Every September-October
  • Highlights Cultural performances, parades, food markets, etc.
  • Contact (+82) 2-2241-2603
  • Location Seongbuk-dong, Seongbuk-gu, Seoul

Each October, Seongbuk-gu celebrates with festivals that reflect the unique characteristics, history, and community spirit of each neighborhood. Notable events include the Samseon Fairy Festival, Jeongneung Willow Leaf Festival, Jangwi Buma Festival, and Seokgwan Uireung Festival, centered around historical sites like the Hanyangdoseong (Seoul City Wall) as well as Jeongneung and Uireung Royal Tombs. Visitors can enjoy diverse activities, including parades, traditional ceremonies like the Buma wedding reenactment, cultural performances, food markets, community bazaars, and interactive experiences.
