Seongbuk Culture Vacation

A festival to experience diverse cultures from around the world through exchanges between locals and international residents.

  • Date Every July-August
  • Highlights Water activities, food market, interactive workshops, cultural performances
  • Contact (+82) 2-2241-2603

Seongbuk Culture Vacation is a summer leisure festival event, offering a unique water play experience close to home for residents who can’t escape the summer heat. It’s a family-friendly event providing fun in a local setting. Held during the summer break at local elementary schools, Seongbuk Culture Vacation allows residents to enjoy a convenient and safe "vacation" in their neighborhood. The festival includes a food market with local groups, cultural performances, and workshops, blending leisure with vibrant cultural and artistic experiences.
